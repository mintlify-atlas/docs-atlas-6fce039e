## Get Your First Evaluation in 2 Minutes

This guide will walk you through evaluating your first answer from start to finish.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Free Gemini API Key">
    The evaluator uses Google's Gemini API to analyze your answers. You'll need a free API key.

    ### How to Get Your API Key

    1. Visit [Google AI Studio](https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey)
    2. Sign in with your Google account
    3. Click **"Create API Key"**
    4. Copy the generated key

    <Note>
      The free tier includes **1,500 requests per day** — more than enough for daily practice.
    </Note>

    ### Add Your API Key to the App

    Once you have your key:

    1. Open the JKPSC Answer Evaluator app
    2. Find the **"Gemini API Key"** section
    3. Paste your key in the input field
    4. Click **"Save API Key"**

    Your key is stored locally in your browser's `localStorage` and never sent to any server (except Google's Gemini API for evaluation).

    ```typescript
    // How the key is stored (from ApiKeyManager.tsx:43)
    localStorage.setItem(STORAGE_KEY, JSON.stringify({ mode: 'plain', key }));
    ```

    <Warning>
      **Keep your API key secure**: Never share it publicly or commit it to version control.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Subject and Marks">
    Choose the subject and question marks that match your answer.

    ### Available Subjects

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="GS1">
        **General Studies Paper 1** — Indian Heritage, Culture, History, Society, Geography

        **Marks Options**: 10 marks (~150 words) or 15 marks (~250 words)

        **Evaluation Focus**:
        - PESTEL+ multidimensional analysis
        - Maps and diagrams emphasis
        - Cultural/historical references
        - Data-driven answers with specific dates, events, and movements
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="GS2">
        **General Studies Paper 2** — Polity, Governance, Social Justice, International Relations

        **Marks Options**: 10 marks (~150 words) or 15 marks (~250 words)

        **Evaluation Focus**:
        - IC5 structure (Introduction → Context → Current Relevance → Conclusion)
        - Constitutional articles and case laws
        - Committee reports and governance frameworks
        - Power and law analysis
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="GS3">
        **General Studies Paper 3** — Economy, Science & Technology, Environment, Internal Security

        **Marks Options**: 10 marks (~150 words) or 15 marks (~250 words)

        **Evaluation Focus**:
        - Data and statistics
        - Policy frameworks and institutions
        - Technical vocabulary
        - Economic/scientific analysis
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="GS4">
        **General Studies Paper 4** — Ethics, Integrity, Aptitude

        **Marks Options**: 10 marks (~150 words) or 20 marks (~250 words)

        **Evaluation Focus**:
        - Ethical theories and thinkers
        - Administrative ethics application
        - Case study analysis (for 20 markers)
        - Behavioral dimensions (PESTEL+)
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Essay">
        **Essay Paper** — Multidimensional thematic essays

        **Marks**: Fixed 125 marks (~1000-1200 words)

        **Evaluation Focus**:
        - 4-section framework (Structure, PESTEL+, Thematic Depth, Expression)
        - Constitutional anchors and scholarly references
        - Topper-level benchmarking
        - Full-circle narrative
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    ### How to Select

    In the **"Subject & Marks"** card:

    1. Click the **"Select Subject"** dropdown
    2. Choose your subject (e.g., GS2 - Polity & Governance)
    3. Click the **"Question Marks"** dropdown
    4. Select the appropriate marks (10/15/20/125)

    <Tip>
      For Essay, marks are automatically set to 125.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Your Answer Sheet">
    Upload your answer as a PDF or multiple images. The AI will automatically extract both the question and your answer.

    ### Supported Formats

    - **PDF** files (up to 10MB)
    - **Images** (JPG, PNG, WebP, up to 10MB each)
    - **Multiple images** (for multi-page answers)

    ### Upload Methods

    **Option 1: Drag & Drop**

    Drag your PDF or images directly into the upload area.

    **Option 2: Click to Browse**

    Click the upload area and select files from your device.

    ### Multiple Image Upload

    If you upload multiple images:

    1. The app shows a **preview grid** of all images
    2. **Drag to reorder** them in the correct sequence
    3. Click **"Process X images with AI"** to continue

    <Note>
      The AI is smart enough to identify the question even if it's not on the first image, but ordering pages correctly improves accuracy.
    </Note>

    ### What Happens Next?

    The app will:

    1. **Extract text** from your PDF (using PDF.js)
    2. **Convert to images** if text extraction yields less than 50 characters (handwritten answers)
    3. **Send to Gemini AI** with a specialized OCR prompt
    4. **Identify the question** (usually in a box or typed text)
    5. **Extract your answer** (handwritten text with structure preserved)

    Here's the actual extraction logic:

    ```typescript
    // From PdfUpload.tsx:157 - AI extraction prompt
    const prompt = `You are processing a handwritten civil services exam answer sheet.
    Your tasks:
    (1) IDENTIFY THE QUESTION: scan ALL images and find the question
    (2) DETERMINE READING ORDER of the answer pages
    (3) OCR all handwritten text in the correct logical order
    
    Return JSON:
    {
      "question": "The full question text",
      "answer": "The full answer text"
    }`;
    ```

    ### Edit Extracted Content

    After extraction, you can:

    - **Review** the extracted question and answer
    - **Edit** either field if the AI missed anything
    - **See word count** for your answer

    <Warning>
      **Upload one question at a time**: Each PDF/image set should contain only ONE question and its answer. Don't upload multiple questions together.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Evaluate Your Answer">
    Once you have:

    - ✅ Added your API key
    - ✅ Selected subject and marks
    - ✅ Uploaded and verified your answer

    Click the big **"Evaluate Answer"** button!

    ### What Happens During Evaluation?

    The evaluation runs in a **Web Worker** (background thread) to keep the UI responsive:

    ```typescript
    // From gemini.ts:514 - Worker-based evaluation
    const worker = new Worker(
      new URL('../workers/evaluationWorker.ts', import.meta.url),
      { type: 'module' }
    );
    worker.postMessage({ apiKey, prompt, marks });
    ```

    The AI:

    1. **Analyzes your answer** against subject-specific rubrics
    2. **Scores 4 categories** (e.g., Technical Depth, PESTEL+ Analysis, Visual Markers, Structure)
    3. **Identifies key points** covered and missing
    4. **Generates improvement tips**
    5. **Creates a model answer structure**

    Evaluation typically takes **10-30 seconds** depending on answer length.

    <Tip>
      Confetti animation plays on successful evaluation! 🎉
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Your Results">
    You'll receive a comprehensive evaluation with 5 tabs:

    ### 1. Overall Score Card

    - **Score**: e.g., 6.5/10 (65%)
    - **Visual progress bar** with color-coded performance
    - **Summary**: 2-3 sentence overview

    ### 2. Category Breakdown

    See your score for each evaluation category:

    - **Technical Depth & Non-Negotiables** (e.g., 3/5 marks)
    - **PESTEL+ Dimensional Analysis** (e.g., 2/3 marks)
    - **Data, Reports & Visual Markers** (e.g., 1/3 marks)
    - **Structure & Expression** (e.g., 3/4 marks)

    Each includes **specific feedback** on what was done well and what was missing.

    ### 3. Key Points Analysis

    **Covered** (green section):
    - Specific terms, articles, cases you mentioned
    - Example: "Article 21", "Puttaswamy judgment", "2nd ARC recommendations"

    **Missing** (red section):
    - Critical points you should have included
    - Example: "Article 14 equality dimension not discussed", "No mention of Sachar Committee"

    ### 4. Strengths

    What you did well:
    - Example: "Good use of IC5 structure with clear introduction"
    - Example: "Effective diagram showing stakeholder mapping"

    ### 5. Improvement Tips

    5 specific, actionable suggestions:
    - Example: "Add Article 15(3) to strengthen gender equality dimension"
    - Example: "Include Kasturirangan Committee report for environmental governance"

    ### 6. Model Answer Structure

    A 250-300 word blueprint showing:
    - Ideal introduction with constitutional hook
    - Body paragraphs with subheadings
    - Key articles/cases/committees to mention
    - Conclusion with way forward

    ### Download PDF Report

    Click **"Download PDF Report"** to save a formatted PDF with:
    - Your question and answer
    - Complete evaluation results
    - All feedback and suggestions

    ```typescript
    // From EvaluationResults.tsx:71 - PDF generation
    const { downloadPdf } = await import('@/lib/pdfGenerator');
    await downloadPdf({ result, subject, marks, question, answer });
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Best Results

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="file-lines" title="Write Clear, Structured Answers">
    - Use subheadings (e.g., INTRODUCTION, CHALLENGES, WAY FORWARD)
    - Number your points for clarity
    - Include diagrams/tables where relevant
    - Mark them clearly so the AI can detect them
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="book" title="Include Technical Terms">
    - Mention specific **Articles** (e.g., Article 21, Article 14)
    - Cite **Case Laws** (e.g., Vishaka Guidelines, Puttaswamy judgment)
    - Reference **Committees** (e.g., 2nd ARC, Sachar Committee)
    - Use **data** and statistics where possible
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="image" title="Upload Quality Images">
    - Ensure good lighting and focus
    - Avoid shadows or glare
    - Keep images under 10MB each
    - Upload pages in the correct order
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="pencil" title="Review Extracted Content">
    - Always check the extracted question and answer
    - Fix OCR errors before evaluating
    - Add any missing content manually
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="triangle-exclamation" title="API Key Error">
    **Error**: "Invalid API key"

    **Solution**:
    - Verify you copied the full key from Google AI Studio
    - Make sure there are no extra spaces
    - Generate a new key if the old one expired
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="hourglass" title="Evaluation Taking Too Long">
    **Issue**: Evaluation stuck or very slow

    **Solution**:
    - Check your internet connection
    - Ensure your answer isn't too long (>400 words for 15-markers)
    - Try refreshing and evaluating again
    - Worker timeout is 2 minutes — it will fallback to main thread
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="file-slash" title="OCR Not Detecting Question/Answer">
    **Issue**: Extracted content is empty or incorrect

    **Solution**:
    - Ensure the question is clearly visible (in a box or typed)
    - Upload clearer images with better lighting
    - Manually type the question/answer in the text areas
    - Make sure images are in the correct order
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Score Seems Too Low">
    **Issue**: Score lower than expected

    **Explanation**:
    - The evaluator enforces a **70% ceiling** (7/10, 10.5/15)
    - UPSC Rank-1 scores are typically 60-65%
    - Generic answers without specific articles/cases are penalized
    - Check "Missing" key points to see what you should add
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
